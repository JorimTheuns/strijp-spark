Strijp-Spark

Brainstormen met heel Strijp-S, voor en door bewoners georganiseerd

We organise get-togethers where you can make an impact in Strijp-S, just by meeting new people and discussing interesting topics.

1. Join us

You sign up to take part, or to volunteer to help out! If we can't fit you in the first round, we will get back to you!

2. Have fun

On the day of the event we host many tiny conversations where people have to work together to react and reply to other groups.

3. Make impact

Dembrane passes the insights and ideas from the day on to organisations in the local area, so they can improve their services.
